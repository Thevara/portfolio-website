Analyzing Gender and Age Aspects of Cyber Bullying Through Online Social Media

Online social media (OSN) has become a prominent platform for different cybercrimes and cyberbullying is one among them. According to National Crime Prevention Council (NCPC), Cyberbullying is stated as the process of using the Internet, cell phones, or other devices to send or post text or images intended to hurt or embarrass another person. In this paper, we address the problem of finding out cyberbullies and categorize them based on their age and gender. The dataset that we use to analyze this information is provided by the Bayzick bullying dataset. DigiBolt - A Digital Locker

Secure storing of important documents such as Aadhar card, Pan card and other such documents, on a mobile device is a difficult task. For secure storing and ease in access to documents we created DigiBolt, a project created in Android Studio which allows you to securely store your documents offline on the phone or on the cloud by scanning these documents once on your mobile device. The documents are stored online using a firebase server which lets you access the files if the user does a login from another device.

Stock market price prediction using Historic Data and Python

In this project, I used the STREAMLIT, FBPROPHET, and YFINANCE libraries to try and forecast the future stock prices of publicly traded companies. The user can select any company ticker from the drop-down list and see the future prediction of the price calculated using historical data. The user will also be able to see a chart plot in detail the possible movement of prices for the next day.
